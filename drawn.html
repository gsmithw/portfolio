About Hand Drawn Art

I first started creating hand drawn work back in 2012. It was the first medium I took on and I mainly started by doodling with pencils. Later on I got interested in using markers, and when I got my confidence up I expanded outside of sketchbooks to larger sizes. The most common size I have used is 18” by 24” and the largest size has been 6’ by 4.’
