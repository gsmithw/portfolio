About Portrait Art

As I expanded into digital art I wanted to take advantage of exploring things that I was unable to do with the hand drawn medium. Something that I struggled to accomplish with hand drawn was drawing people. This has been something that I have been able to explore since in the digital realm. This is a collection of friends and musicians that I like, and scenes from movies.
